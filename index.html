Why use Flutter?

  • Hot Reload

    The changes made by the developers can be seen instantaneously with Hot Reload.
  • Cross-Platform Development CPD saves time, energy and money.
  • Accessible Native Features and SDKsFlutter’s native codes, third-party integrations, and platform APIs.
  • Widgets Capable of developing customizable and complex widgets.
  • IDE's SupportThe Google Flutter is also supportive for an extensive array of IDEs that include Xcode, Visual Studio Code and Android Studio.
  • Fast Performance Flutter means faster & more dynamic mobile app development.
  • Less testingIf you have the same app for 2 platforms, it means less testing!
  • Package repository(pub.dev) Download and import packages on the go!!

Flutter Widgets

Stateful vs Stateless Widgets

  • Stateful: They are the widgets which can change their state multiple times and can be redrawn on to the screen any number of times while the app is in action.
  • Stateless: In simple words, Stateless widgets cannot change their state during the runtime of the app, which means the widgets cannot be redrawn while the app is in action.
